Chy's Homestyle Chicken Mashed Potato Bowl

By: Chy Ms. Master Chef

A mouthwatering and delicious bowl that tastes just like the famous kfc chicken and mashed potato bowl. "It's a delight and tastes just right" .

Ingredients

  • 1 20-30 oz package frozen breaded popcorn chicken
  • 2 24 oz packages Bob Evans Original Mashed Potatoes
  • 1 14.5 oz can whole kernel corn
  • 1 cup shredded colby-jack cheese
  • 1 cup herbal-vegetable gravy

Instructions

  1. Cook the frozen popcorn chicken according to package directions, preferably in the oven or air fryer so they are nice and crispy.
  2. Meanwhile warm up the canned corn in a small pot over medium heat.
  3. Heat the Bob Evans Original Mashed Potatoes according to package instructions and divide into 4 bowls.
  4. Warm up the herbal-vegetable gravy according to package instructions as well.
  5. Top the hot mashed potatoes with ¼ cup of the shredded cheese, ⅓ cup of the corn kernels (drained), and a few strips of the crispy chicken strips.
  6. Drizzle the top of the bowl with the hot herbal-vegetable gravy and enjoy!
